Humberside Fire and Rescue Service

Retained Firefighter

As a retained firefighter with Humberside Fire and Rescue Service, I served the community in emergency response situations. This role required dedication, quick decision-making, and the ability to work effectively under pressure while maintaining the highest safety standards.

Key Responsibilities

  • Emergency response to fire incidents
  • Road traffic accident rescue operations
  • Community safety and fire prevention
  • Equipment maintenance and testing
  • Training and skills development
  • Working as part of emergency response team

Skills and Training

  • Fire suppression techniques and tactics
  • Rescue operations and first aid
  • Emergency vehicle operation
  • Risk assessment and safety procedures
  • Team leadership and communication
  • Working under pressure and time constraints
  • Community engagement and education

Personal Impact

This role developed my leadership abilities, crisis management skills, and reinforced the importance of serving the community. The experience taught me valuable lessons about teamwork, responsibility, and maintaining composure in challenging situations.
