Manufacturing Experience

My time at Basemetal Synergy provided valuable experience in non-ferrous metal processing. Smelting base metals such as tin from raw dross or unpurified ingots and producing IMI bars for solder which would be pressed through a die to produce solder wire. There was also semi-precious metal smelting such as Indium and also low grade gold such as you would find on curcuit boards.
