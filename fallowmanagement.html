Fallow Management LTD

Security, Cleaning and Caretaking (soft Services)

Fallow Management provided the building management industry with soft services such as, security, cleaning and caretaking. my place of work was Brunswick Point, a former Yorkshire Bank building situated in the city of Leeds. I was initially employed as the buildings caretaker, responsible for the overall basic maintenance and then, once I passed my SIA training course, I picked up reception and general security duties as well as my caretaking role. As the caretaker, I was also the cleaning manager looking after a small team of cleaners.

Key Activities

  • The safety of everyone in the building
  • Ensuring the building is in the best of repair
  • Clean and safe for all occupants and visitors
  • Ensuring my staff were presented with the correct equipment
  • Quantify and restock of cleaning supplies
  • Quality assurance and monitoring
