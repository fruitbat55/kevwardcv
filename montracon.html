Refrigerated Trailer Manufacturing

Montracon LTD (Market Weighton) is a leading manufacturer of refrigerated trailers at both 13.5m and 9m lengths plus cold stores. My immediate role there was as a machinist operating a beam saw which trimmed the panels for the trailers' sides, roofs and floors to the correct length and width. My last role before taking redundancy was as a panel trimmer which involved trimming the likes of bulkheads and doors with hand tools such as circular saws.

Key Activities

  • Operating machinery
  • Ensuring the panels are cut to the correct sizes
  • Finishing the panels ready for installation
  • Trimming smaller panels to spec
  • Ensuring the hand tools are in good and safe working order

Skills Developed

  • Use of different machinery
  • Utilisation of hand tools
  • Safe use of equipment such as overhead cranes
  • Working within time constraints
